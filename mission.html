MISSION AND VISION

The aim of the Petroleum Division is to support the Government’s effort to develop the Nation’s discovered and potentially discoverable petroleum resources by promoting, monitoring and regulating all activities directly related to the exploration, development and production of petroleum in Papua New Guinea.

Department of Petroleum and Energy (DPE) primary role is to regulate the Petroleum and Energy sectors in PNG. It is mandated to regulate the fossil fuel (Oil and Gas) as well as non – fossil fuel (solar, hydro, biomass, wind, geothermal, etc.) energy sectors as well as the roles and responsibilities of all important stakeholders. The Department is now pursuing to develop the Petrochemical Industry.

The 2015 Updates highlights the status of all Oil and Gas projects both existing and proposed and other Department’s institutional projects undertaken by my Department. The status of each project in this update reflects the Department’s efforts in regulating and delivering all these important Petroleum projects alongside the Project Developers.

We will continue to provide project updates and presents it as part of the Department’s role in disseminating information on Petroleum Projects developments in 2015 and beyond. So far the Department have issued a total of ten (10) Petroleum Development Licenses for petroleum projects and other various Petroleum Licenses to project developers in the country.

Thanks to the continued efforts and support of the State and all stakeholders that are directly involved with the sector. With such support, we can overcome difficulties and issues and successfully deliver these all important Projects. The Way forwards are captured in this update to indicate pathways and highlights measures towards successfully delivering each Projects. 

Oil and Gas projects are revenue generated projects that have and will continue to bring economic benefits into the country. These projects are our future. They are essential for the functioning of our economy, for growth, jobs and increase investments.

OBJECTIVES I

  • To actively promote the petroleum exploration potential of PNG through production of geological reports on basin prospectively.
  • To monitor all of the petroleum exploration and development activities of all parties within Papua New Guinea.
  • To administer Petroleum Prospecting, Retention, Development and Pipeline Licenses.
  • To access the results of exploration, development and production activities of all parties within Papua New Guinea. 
  • To advise the Department and other arms of Government on technical aspects of exploration, development and production activity, results of activity, levels of production and reserves and feasibility of projects. 
  • To ensure the receipt of, and approving the dissemination of petroleum exploration, development and production data, reports and samples.

OBJECTIVES II

  • To ensure training and development of specialized citizen manpower in collaboration with the Support Services Division. 
  • To bring about understanding issues associated with petroleum exploration, development and production by all parties concerned particularly landowners.
  • To coordinate and lead policy developers in Departmental, industry and public forums.
  • To evaluate the economic, financial and legal implications of exploration and development of petroleum resources for the State, Provincial Government and landowners.
  • To coordinate and effect the negotiation and drafting of agreements between the State, project developers, Provincial Governments and landowners relating to petroleum and exploration and development activities.
